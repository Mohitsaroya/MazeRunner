Draw the maze bounding box in {\ttfamily win}. 

Draws a rectangular border for the maze area inside the provided {\ttfamily WINDOW}. Interior cells are initialized to spaces. This function does not populate level-\/specific walls or items; those are placed by {\ttfamily maze1} or {\ttfamily maze2} after the border is drawn.
